About Us

Welcome to this historical exploration of photography, a timeline curated with precision and passion by Harold Luna. As a dedicated assignment for a course at New York University, this project seeks to encapsulate the significant milestones that photography has seen since its inception.

The timeline begins with the camera obscura, traverses through the development of film and the birth of digital photography, and reaches into the modern era of smartphones and computational photography. Each entry on this timeline is a testament to the ingenuity and creativity of pioneers who have transformed the way we capture and interpret our visual world.

Harold Luna, an avid photographer and history enthusiast, crafted this timeline to not only fulfill an academic requirement but also to share his passion for photography with a wider audience. Through this educational journey, visitors are invited to witness the evolution of an art form that continues to shape our society and personal lives.

This website serves as both an informative resource and a source of inspiration. Whether you are a student, a professional photographer, or simply someone who appreciates the art of photography, this timeline is a tribute to the marvels of human innovation and the relentless pursuit of capturing beauty and truth.
